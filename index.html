About Sindhu Global Foundation Trust

Welcome to Sindhu Global Foundation Trust, a beacon of hope and change dedicated to uplifting communities across the globe. Established in 2015, we are a non-profit organization committed to creating a lasting impact through compassion, innovation, and collaboration.

Our Mission

At Sindhu Global Foundation Trust, our mission is to empower underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable opportunities. We believe in fostering self-reliance and building a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Through our initiatives, we aim to bridge gaps, break barriers, and unite people for a common cause—positive change.

What We Do
  • Education for All: We support children from marginalized communities by providing scholarships, school supplies, and access to quality education.
  • Healthcare Initiatives: Our mobile clinics and health camps bring essential medical services to remote areas, ensuring no one is left behind.
  • Community Development: From building sustainable infrastructure to offering vocational training, we empower communities to become self-sufficient.
  • Disaster Relief: In times of crisis, we provide immediate aid, including food, shelter, and medical support, to affected regions.
Our Impact

Over the past decade, we’ve touched the lives of over 50,000 individuals, partnered with 100+ NGOs, and raised more than ₹1 crore to support our causes. Together, we are building a legacy of change—one step at a time.

Join us in our journey to create a better tomorrow. Together, we can make a difference!
